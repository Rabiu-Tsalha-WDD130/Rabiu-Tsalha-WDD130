WHITE WATER RAFTING

White water rafting in Nigeria, like elsewhere, serves a variety of purposes, including recreation, tourism, and even some forms of team building. It's an adventure activity that combines physical exertion, camaraderie, and the enjoyment of nature. For those seeking thrill, it offers an adrenaline rush, while for others, it's a way to experience the wilderness and connect with the river's beauty.

History

White water rafting as a sport in Nigeria has a relatively short history compared to its development in other parts of the world. While the Bussa Rapids on the Niger River have long been a natural feature, the development of rafting as a recreational activity is more recent.
